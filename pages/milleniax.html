First of all, the team at MilleniaX want to help younger generations(millenials) achieve things from an early age, gaining work experience and developing abilities through the path. This website/app connects students (high school, college , university) to the employer( from McDonald's cashier to an internship at BMO). It is important to emphasize that the website will be extremely easy to use and at the same time an amazing design. You will create a profile with a single basic resume that can fit in most average jobs(specific information will be done directly with employer) and with just one click you will be able to apply to several jobs near you or any job you want. The employer will be able to compare and contrast applicants through the portal and agree on terms. All in a safe way that benefits both parties. Above all, MilleniaX's ultimate goal is to create opportunity for younger and present generations helping them strive for perfection in order to create a better world.
